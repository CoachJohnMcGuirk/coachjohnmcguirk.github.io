about
me

I'm a Video Artist who currently resides with my amazing wife and kids in beautiful Colorado. I currently work at the Amazon Games San Diego Studio and I've previously worked for LEGO, NetDevil, and Rally Software as well as Charlex and Quiet Man, two top-tier visual effects companies in New York City. I've also done freelance projects for Amazon, Walt Disney Imagineering, and Backflip Studios.

I'm grateful for the variety of projects I've had a chance to work on and I'm excited for whatever the future may hold. I absolutely love games, animation, and motion graphics and I always welcome the chance to learn and problem solve!

In my spare time...or what little of it I have, I love playing video games, watching movies, and practicing drawing and painting.
